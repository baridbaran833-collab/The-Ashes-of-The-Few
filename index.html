Los Angeles, 2026

ASHES OF THE FEW

"Some builders cause wreckage. Some wreckers protect what's left. And some men are both before breakfast."

Neo-noir crime saga Interactive web-comic Part 1: The City That Looked Away

The city is beautiful in the way a blade is beautiful: polished, bright, and always capable of cutting someone open.

Main Characters

The Few

Three men orbit the same broken city: one kills by code, one rules by system, and one turns wounds into architecture.

MAXTON

Daytime forensic lab owner. Nighttime serial killer.

  • Protect innocents at all costs.
  • Never harm innocents intentionally.
  • Do not get caught.
  • Eliminate those who repeatedly prey on innocent people when the system fails.

KANE GREY

Youngest self-made billionaire. Leader of 5T. Believes systems should be improved rather than bypassed.

Skyscrapers, boardrooms, luxury, control, and the awful weight of city surveillance.

SCARPER

Master manipulator. Acid-scarred criminal mastermind. Believes morality is hypocrisy.

Abandoned factories, acid symbolism, industrial decay, broken mirrors, and shadows that learned to speak.

A city can hold two truths at once if one of them is bloody enough.

Themes Section

What Survives

Justice vs Vengeance

The story refuses to make righteousness clean when justice arrives through blood.

Indifference Creates Monsters

The city does not simply fail people. It teaches them what failure costs.

Trauma and Identity

Each man becomes a doctrine built around the wound he could not escape.

Corruption

Power hides inside procedure, philanthropy, loopholes, and polite rooms with expensive glass.

Moral Ambiguity

No one here stands in clean light for long enough to become simple.

Redemption

The ending does not forgive easily. It asks who is willing to carry what remains.
